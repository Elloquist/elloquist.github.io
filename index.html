About Elloquist

Elloquist is a science and culture media brand founded by Ellen Hermance, publishing YouTube videos, newsletters on Substack, and essays on Medium. Operated by Elloquist LLC, the brand covers epigenetics, houseplant science, anthropology, pop culture, music, memory, and the everyday wonders of a well-tended life.

From epigenetics to houseplants, from pop culture to anthropology: every piece of content is an invitation to look closer at the world around you and find it more fascinating than you expected.

"The science of living things. The stories hidden in culture. The quiet wonder of a well-tended life."

Elloquist bridges the gap between academic curiosity and everyday wonder, making complex ideas beautiful and accessible. Whether you're here for the plant science, the cultural deep-dives, or simply to see the world with fresh eyes, you're in the right place.

What Elloquist Explores

The Science of Living Things

From epigenetics and cell biology to the hidden intelligence of plants: the living world is stranger and more beautiful than most textbooks let on. Each video unpacks a piece of biology that rewards a second look.

Stories Hidden in Culture

Anthropology, folklore, pop culture, and social history reveal why humans think and behave the way we do. Every tradition and trend has a deeper story. Elloquist finds it.

Music, Memory & the Mind

Why does a song trigger such vivid memory? How does sound become emotionally anchored to a moment? The neuroscience and psychology behind our most distinctly human experiences.

The Well-Tended Life

Houseplants, biophilic design, slow living, and the science behind why surrounding yourself with living things genuinely changes how your brain functions and how you feel.
